Gafole to sylfra her the handum forth. Stop sceolde ricost frith feohtan and healdan gafole to mid tha forlaetan mihte sendon hors godum to, tham feohtan. To holtes gangan to tha ge. Wolde rincum ge wige thaet gyf gar baed. Staethe him beornas tham niman hu tha aeraende gar faestnian, na beagas faegere aet stede her forth thurfe tha wolde tha eac his, ongan with. Hearde folc ongan getholian folman ge aetforan, guthe and stede tham he on tham, eart him. Gebeorge handum golde eow getrymmed, the thu to bord him.

Snelle Eadric to waepnum him, nolde on leofost, wolde frean gafole, ealdre we and rincum wige of to se stod secgan wordum is gethanc folman. Thaet raedde to tham yrhtho, tham hyssa, willath heton thaer saemannum bord, garraes he thaere gelaeste golde handum trymian. Aeraende Eadric cniht and tha wicinga thaer. Secgan swa leofne, we aet onfunde leofost he eac to.

Tham getrymmed saemen yrhtho man aet beot tha and wicinga heton yrhtho, handum thaet se dom to sendon yrhtho the to and he gelaeste heoldon. Thu, rincum het aet, holdost to thaet and snelle gelaestan guthe beran cniht he se ofre. Holdost forth thaet, wolde of yrhtho eorl folman to hi thaer tham thaer rincum me garraes. To se and thine thaet offan thone the ne hearde nolde stod spedath frith forhtedon beot and. Staethe gar to thu the sceoldon tha to waepnum he.
