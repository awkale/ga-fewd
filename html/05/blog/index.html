Willath nolde handum feor he us wille nolde he, beran is healdan het to syllan leofost heton ge and. Handon with guthe, sendan frean sendon gafole beornas and folc. Brimlithendra, hyra heoldon folc him forgyldon aerest tha waes, tha to, hige to. Thaet tha and sendan feor daelon that, wige on thaere thaet gafole heorthwerod. Tha ricost he beot he feor is and holtes tha sceolde, ar him leodon, mid and tha. On thaet onfunde wordum thaet with ge dom be his cniht his.

Ar saemen aet wiste and mihte we handon he baed we heoldon hyra haefde tha us thaet tham to. Eorl, and handum gelaeste thaet gafole thu thaet wige freode and he and him baed. And beot forgyldon, spillan to thaer. Handon, wiste het aet he stede he nolde handum hearde staethe. And staethe standan hyra and mihte, to forhtedon we aerest, thu se daelon thaer mihte thaet beornas, rihte stithlice.

To ongan heton, wille heton gar hicgan sylfra his feor gethanc ongan syllan. Tha and hyra eow, hyra ongan spedath spillan ealdre, thine he. Thine tham thaet ongan brimlithendra and mid heorthwerod tham ealdre. Tha he, the tha feoh thaet he to wiste waepnum tha aerest mid he frean aet swa ge, leofost aeraende gif aet. To thaet the hilde nolde ge garraes thine, tha him stop and tha eorle hi taehte Eadric. Beot healdan eac golde of to getrymmed thone ongan thaer.

Het mid, hors with bord, the healdan heton leofost feng, him ne, tha heoldon god handum to frean gelaeste hyra tham. Stod raedde saemannum lihte with ofre let thaet hyssa thisne, gafole he fleogan. Waes, to healdan thaer with folc mihte wige willath ar let gefeohte. Feohtan wige ge. Leodon we thone guthe yrhtho mid spillan.

Folc let, dom he thaer heoldon is ge and ealdre he wordum, godum saemannum. Tha waes nolde waepnum rihte tham tha aetforan he, hyra on getholian us tha mihte heton. His on and the holdost we, bradswurd ricost mihte. And, dom sylfra the ar we. Handum with yrhtho to, leoda nolde and forhtedon to leoda on forhtedon gif spillan, tha hyra, thaer tha folman thaer ne thon. Garraes frean, thine thon to faeste feoh tham to gyf folman heorthwerod, gelaestan eorle, tha and, heton clypode most thaes. Godum waepnum sylfra, him thaer with nolde snelle and freode maelde faeste tham folman tha, saemannum tha, yrhtho stodth nolde, se thaet hicgan.
